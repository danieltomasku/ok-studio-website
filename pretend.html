The Challenge

Local furniture maker and environments designer Matthew Philip Williams wanted to grow his practice, complimenting his elegant designs with branding that more closeely spoke to his personality and aesthetic. The new brand needed to allow for his company to grow as well as reflect the high brow meets blue collar ethos behind the work.

The Solution

We began by building a strong brand foundation including competitive audit, market positioning, brand character and target audience profiles. This was followed by the naming process which embraced the accidental, yet serendipitous founding of his company.

Furniture, spaces and objects oh my!
We leveraged colors, shapes and patterns found in Pretend Company's catalogue of designs. Friendly sans serifs combine with iconographic furniture to create a fun, yet elegant aesthetic.
