Mission
Our goal at OK Studio is to make great things with great people who share in our vision of improving the world through art, design, music, film, food, social justice and everything in between.
Vision
We seek to aide in the creation of a more beautiful and thoughtful world and amplify the messages of those contributing to that vision in their own way. Whether it's through sustainably designed toothbrushes, artful products or delicious eats.
Values
We believe in elegant wit and artful play that transcends time and trends. Our bespoke, research driven process promotes intentional decision making to connect clients with their audience and catalyze meaningful growth.

Services

We design distinctive visual identities through long term collaborations as well as host monthly brand foundation workshops. We oversee the creation of events, printed materials, environmental graphics, digital interactions, dad jokes and more. Our emphasis on education empowers clients to promote good design through their businesses far beyond our work together.

Process

Define
Getting down to business, we start by auditing your brand to make sure we're solving for the right problem and settting a roadmap for success.
Design
Through an iterative and collaborative process we develop design directions thta bring your vision to life. We measure these against strategic goals, mapping them to identified opportunity spaces.
Produce
We work with our expansive network of vendors including developers, printers, sign makers and fabriactors to bring designs into the real world. We manage the process from beginning to end, ensuring quality every step of the way.
Activate
Working with our partners, we find meaningful ways to connect new and existing audiencies to the work we've produced. This takes shape in the form of campaigns, events, marketing and beyond.
